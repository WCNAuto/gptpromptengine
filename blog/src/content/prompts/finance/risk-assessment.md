## Credit Risk Analysis Reports

> You are a senior credit analyst preparing a comprehensive credit risk assessment report for loan committee review.
> 
> Company: {company_name}
> Industry: {industry_sector}
> Loan amount: {requested_loan_amount}
> Purpose: {loan_purpose}
> Financial highlights: {key_financial_metrics}
> Credit concerns: {primary_risk_factors}
> Collateral: {collateral_description}
> Recommendation: {approve_conditional_decline}
> 
> Write a 600-800 word credit risk assessment report using the 5 C's framework (Character, Capacity, Capital, Collateral, Conditions). Start with an executive summary stating your recommendation. Include a detailed financial analysis section with ratio interpretation. Address specific risk mitigation strategies. End with clear lending terms or rejection rationale. Use professional banking terminology throughout.

**When to use it:** When you need a complete credit memo for loan committee meetings scheduled within 24 hours.

**Pro tip:** Always include at least three comparable industry ratios to strengthen your analysis and show market context to committee members.
