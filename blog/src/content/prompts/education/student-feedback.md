## Mid-Term Progress Reports

> You are writing a mid-term progress report for a student showing mixed academic performance.
> 
> Student: {student_name}
> Grade/Year: {grade_level}
> Subject: {subject_area}
> Current grade: {current_grade_letter}
> Strengths (2-3 specific examples): {academic_strengths}
> Areas needing improvement: {improvement_areas}
> Recent assessment scores: {test_scores_or_assignments}
> Attendance pattern: {attendance_description}
> Parent contact preference: {formal / conversational}
> 
> Write a 200-250 word mid-term progress report. Start with one specific strength. Address improvement areas with concrete next steps. Include one actionable suggestion for home support. End with encouragement tied to the student's potential.

**When to use it:** When you're halfway through a grading period and need to update parents on students who aren't clearly excelling or failing.

**Pro tip:** Always include a specific recent example in the strengths section - it shows you're paying individual attention and gives parents something concrete to celebrate.

**Pro tip:** Frame declining effort as "ready for more challenging work" rather than "not trying hard enough" - it preserves self-esteem while addressing the issue.

## Frequently Asked Questions

### How can AI prompts help teachers write more effective student feedback reports?
AI prompts provide structured templates that ensure comprehensive coverage of student performance while maintaining consistent professional language. They help teachers organize observations into coherent reports and suggest specific language for addressing sensitive issues diplomatically.

### What information should teachers gather before using AI prompts for student feedback?
Teachers need specific examples of student work, concrete performance data, behavioral observations with dates and contexts, intervention strategies attempted, and clear goals for the feedback. The more specific the input data, the more useful and personalized the generated report will be.

### How do AI-generated student feedback reports compare to traditionally written ones?
AI-generated reports using well-designed prompts often provide more comprehensive and consistent coverage of student performance areas. They help ensure important elements aren't forgotten and maintain professional tone throughout, while still allowing teachers to add personal observations and specific examples that reflect individual knowledge of each student.
