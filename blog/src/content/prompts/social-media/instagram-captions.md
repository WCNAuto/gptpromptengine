## Product Launch Announcements

> You are a social media manager announcing a new product launch on Instagram.
> 
> Product: {product_name}
> Key benefit: {main_customer_benefit}
> Launch date: {launch_date}
> Price point: {price_or_price_range}
> Target audience: {primary_demographic}
> Brand voice: {professional / playful / luxury / approachable}
> Special offer: {discount_code_or_early_bird_offer}
> Call to action: {shop_now / pre_order / learn_more / sign_up}
> 
> Write a 120-150 word Instagram caption that opens with excitement about the launch, explains the key benefit in customer-focused language, includes the special offer, and ends with a clear call to action. Include 3-5 relevant hashtags at the end.

**When to use it:** When your product team just confirmed the launch details and you need the announcement post ready for tomorrow morning's scheduled release.

**Pro tip:** Always test your discount codes before posting. Nothing kills launch momentum like followers getting error messages at checkout.
