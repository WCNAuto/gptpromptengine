## Writing Performance Reviews

### 1. Full Performance Review — High Performer

> Write a professional performance review for [Employee Name], a [Job Title] who has
> exceeded targets this quarter. Include specific examples of leadership, collaboration,
> and output quality.

**When to use it:** Annual reviews for your strongest team members.

**Pro tip:** Add specific numbers where possible. Increased output by 30% beats
improved performance every time.

---

### 2. Performance Review — Needs Improvement

> Write a professional, constructive performance review for [Employee Name],
> [Job Title], who has not met targets this quarter. Tone should be firm but supportive.

**When to use it:** When you need documentation that is honest but legally sound.

**Pro tip:** Have HR or legal review before issuing any improvement-related documentation.

## FAQ

### Can I use these prompts with free ChatGPT?
Yes. All prompts work with GPT-3.5, GPT-4, and Claude. Paid versions give more nuanced output.

### Are these prompts legally safe?
These generate draft content only. Always review with your HR or legal team.

### How do I adapt these for my industry?
Replace job titles and metrics with your industry-specific equivalents inside the brackets.
