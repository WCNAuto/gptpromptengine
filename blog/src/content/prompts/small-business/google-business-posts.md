## Seasonal Promotions and Events

> You are writing a Google Business Profile post for a limited-time seasonal promotion.
> 
> Business: {business_name}
> Promotion: {specific_offer_or_discount}
> Valid dates: {start_date} to {end_date}
> Target audience: {primary_customer_type}
> Location: {city_and_neighborhood}
> Call to action: {book_now / call_today / visit_us / order_online}
> Urgency factor: {limited_quantity / first_50_customers / while_supplies_last}
> Business hours: {today_or_weekend_hours}
> 
> Write a 120 to 150 word Google Business Profile post that opens with the discount percentage or dollar amount, includes the urgency factor in the first sentence, mentions the neighborhood for local relevance, and ends with a clear call to action and your hours. Use conversational tone with one question to engage readers.

**When to use it:** Monday morning when launching your weekly or seasonal special, or 48 hours before a promotion expires.

**Pro tip:** Posts with specific dollar amounts ($15 off) perform better than percentages (20% off) for local searches under $100.
