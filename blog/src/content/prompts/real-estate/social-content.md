## Property Showcase Posts

> You are a real estate agent creating a property showcase post for social media.
> 
> Property address: {property_address}
> Price: {listing_price}
> Key features: {three_standout_features}
> Target buyer type: {first_time_buyers / luxury_buyers / investors / downsizers}
> Neighborhood highlight: {local_amenity_or_school}
> Your agency: {agency_name}
> Platform: {instagram / facebook / linkedin}
> 
> Write a 150-200 word social media post that opens with a compelling hook about the property's best feature. Include price and address. End with a clear call-to-action for bookings. Use 3-5 relevant hashtags but avoid overuse.

**When to use it:** Every time you get a new listing and need to announce it across your social channels within the first 24 hours.

**Pro tip:** Lead with emotion-driven features like "morning coffee on the deck overlooking the lake" rather than technical specs like "composite decking material."

## Market Insights and Tips

> You are sharing local market data with your social media audience.
> 
> Time period: {month_or_quarter}
> Area covered: {neighborhood_or_city}
> Average sale price: {average_price}
> Days on market: {average_days}
> Market trend: {up / down / stable}
> Key insight: {most_important_takeaway}
> Advice for buyers/sellers: {specific_actionable_tip}
> Data source: {MLS / local_board}
> 
> Create a 160-220 word market update post. Start with the headline number that matters most. Explain what this means for buyers and sellers in plain language. End with one specific action people should take. Make data digestible, not overwhelming.

**When to use it:** First week of each month when new market data becomes available from your MLS.

**Pro tip:** Focus on one key metric rather than overwhelming followers with multiple statistics - choose what matters most to your typical client.

## Frequently Asked Questions

### What makes ChatGPT prompts effective for real estate social media content?
Effective prompts include specific variables you can customize, clear length constraints, and produce finished posts rather than templates. They save time by creating content you can publish immediately with minimal editing.

### How often should real estate agents post social media content?
Aim for 4-6 posts per week across platforms, mixing property showcases, market insights, and client stories. Consistency matters more than frequency - better to post 3 times weekly consistently than 7 times sporadically.

### Which social media platforms work best for real estate marketing?
Instagram and Facebook remain strongest for visual property content, while LinkedIn excels for market insights and professional networking. Focus on 2-3 platforms rather than spreading thin across all channels.
