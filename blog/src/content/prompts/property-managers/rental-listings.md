## Luxury Property Descriptions

> You are a luxury real estate copywriter creating a high-end rental listing.
> 
> Property type: {property_type}
> Location: {neighborhood}, {city}
> Rent: ${monthly_rent}
> Square footage: {sq_ft} sq ft
> Bedrooms/bathrooms: {bedrooms} bed, {bathrooms} bath
> Luxury features: {three_to_five_premium_amenities}
> Building amenities: {building_features}
> Target tenant: {ideal_renter_profile}
> Tone: {sophisticated / warm / exclusive}
> 
> Write a 250-300 word luxury rental listing that opens with an aspirational lifestyle statement, highlights the three most compelling features in the second paragraph, and closes with a subtle urgency driver. Use elevated language without being pretentious. Include rent and key details naturally within the flow.

**When to use it:** When you're listing properties above market rate and need to justify premium pricing with compelling lifestyle positioning.

**Pro tip:** If the AI uses too many superlatives, add "avoid words like 'stunning,' 'breathtaking,' and 'amazing'" to your constraint section.

## Budget-Friendly Properties

> You are writing a listing for affordable housing that emphasizes value without sounding cheap.
> 
> Property type: {unit_style}
> Monthly rent: ${rent_amount}
> Included utilities: {utilities_covered}
> Neighborhood: {area_name}
> Transportation: {public_transit_access}
> Recent updates: {improvements_made}
> Application process: {requirements_and_timeline}
> Deposit amount: ${security_deposit}
> Pet policy: {pet_information}
> 
> Create a 180-220 word value-focused listing that opens with total monthly cost including utilities, highlights recent improvements, and emphasizes location benefits. Use straightforward language and end with clear application instructions.

**When to use it:** For below-market properties where tenants are cost-sensitive and need transparent pricing upfront.

**Pro tip:** Always lead with total monthly costs - budget-conscious renters want to know their real expenses before reading feature descriptions.
