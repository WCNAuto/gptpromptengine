## Viral Title Generation

> You are a YouTube growth strategist creating viral titles for a specific video topic.

> Channel name: {channel_name}
> Video topic: {main_topic}
> Target audience: {audience_description}
> Content type: {tutorial / review / reaction / vlog / challenge}
> Current subscriber count: {subscriber_count}
> Competitor channels doing well: {competitor_examples}
> Emotional hook needed: {curiosity / urgency / controversy / surprise / fear}

> Generate 15 YouTube titles under 60 characters each. Include 3 titles using numbers, 3 with power words (shocking, secret, ultimate), 3 question-based titles, 3 comparison titles, and 3 emotional hooks. Format as a numbered list with estimated click-through rate reasoning for each.

**When to use it:** When you've filmed content but can't decide on a title that will actually get clicks.

**Pro tip:** Test your top 3 titles with YouTube's A/B testing feature. Titles with odd numbers (7, 13, 21) often outperform even numbers.

## Thumbnail Concept Development

> You are a YouTube thumbnail designer creating concepts for high-performing thumbnails.

> Video title: {video_title}
> Main topic: {core_subject}
> Channel style: {minimalist / bold / colorful / professional / quirky}
> Target demographic: {age_and_interests}
> Competitor thumbnails: {describe_what_others_are_doing}
> Key visual elements available: {props_people_locations}
> Emotion to convey: {curiosity / excitement / shock / trust / urgency}
> Text overlay needed: {yes_or_no}

> Create 5 detailed thumbnail concepts. For each concept, describe: main visual focus, color scheme, text placement and font style, facial expression (if applicable), background elements, and why it would outperform competitors. Include specific composition rules (rule of thirds, contrast, etc.).

**When to use it:** When you need thumbnail concepts before a photoshoot or when current thumbnails aren't getting clicks.

**Pro tip:** Test thumbnails at phone screen size. If you can't read text or identify faces at thumbnail size, redesign.
