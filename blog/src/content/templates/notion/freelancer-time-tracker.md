Whether you're a graphic designer, writer, consultant, or any type of freelancer, this template provides everything you need to stay organized and profitable.

## Template Sections and Fields

### 1. Projects Database
**Purpose**: Central hub for all your freelance projects

**Fields**:
- **Project Name** (Title): Unique identifier for each project
- **Client** (Relation): Links to client database
- **Status** (Select): Not Started, In Progress, Under Review, Completed, On Hold
- **Project Type** (Select): Website Design, Content Writing, Consulting, Marketing, Development, Other
- **Start Date** (Date): Project commencement date
- **Deadline** (Date): Final delivery date
- **Budget** (Number): Total project value
- **Hourly Rate** (Number): Rate per hour for time-based projects
- **Total Hours** (Rollup): Automatically calculated from time entries
- **Total Earned** (Formula): Calculates earnings based on hours and rate
- **Progress** (Select): 0%, 25%, 50%, 75%, 100%
- **Priority** (Select): Low, Medium, High, Urgent
- **Description** (Text): Detailed project scope and requirements
- **Deliverables** (Text): List of expected outputs
- **Notes** (Text): Additional project information

### 2. Time Tracking Database
**Purpose**: Log all work sessions with precise time tracking

**Fields**:
- **Date** (Date): When the work was performed
- **Project** (Relation): Links to projects database
- **Task Description** (Title): Brief description of work done
- **Start Time** (Date with time): Work session start
- **End Time** (Date with time): Work session end
- **Duration** (Formula): Automatically calculates time spent
- **Hourly Rate** (Rollup): Pulls rate from related project
- **Earnings** (Formula): Calculates session earnings
- **Billable** (Checkbox): Whether time can be invoiced
- **Category** (Select): Design, Writing, Research, Meeting, Admin, Revision
- **Notes** (Text): Additional details about the work

### 3. Clients Database
**Purpose**: Comprehensive client relationship management

**Fields**:
- **Client Name** (Title): Company or individual name
- **Contact Person** (Text): Primary point of contact
- **Email** (Email): Primary contact email
- **Phone** (Phone): Contact phone number
- **Company** (Text): Client's company name
- **Address** (Text): Billing address
- **Status** (Select): Active, Inactive, Prospect, Former

- **Preferred Communication** (Select): Email, Phone, Slack, Teams
- **Total Projects** (Rollup): Count of projects for this client
- **Total Earned** (Rollup): Total earnings from this client
- **Notes** (Text): Important client information
- **Contracts** (Files): Uploaded contract documents

### 4. Invoices Database
**Purpose**: Track billing and payment status

**Fields**:
- **Invoice Number** (Title): Unique invoice identifier
- **Client** (Relation): Links to clients database
- **Project** (Relation): Associated project
- **Invoice Date** (Date): When invoice was sent
- **Due Date** (Date): Payment due date

## FAQ

### Q: How do I handle projects with different billing structures?
**A**: The template supports both hourly and fixed-rate projects. For fixed-rate projects, enter the total budget in the Budget field and leave the hourly rate blank. For hourly projects, set your rate in the Hourly Rate field. The template will automatically calculate earnings based on tracked time for hourly projects, while fixed-rate projects will show the full budget amount.

### Q: Can I track non-billable time like admin work or business development?
**A**: Yes! Each time entry has a "Billable" checkbox that you can uncheck for non-billable work. This allows you to track all your time while keeping billable and non-billable hours separate for accurate client billing. You can create projects for internal work like "Business Development" or "Administration" to categorize this time.

### Q: How do I generate invoices from my tracked time?
**A**: While this template tracks all the data you need for invoicing, you'll need to export the information to your preferred invoicing tool. You can filter the Time Tracking database by project and billable status, then copy the relevant data. The Projects database shows total billable hours and earnings, making it easy to create accurate invoices.
