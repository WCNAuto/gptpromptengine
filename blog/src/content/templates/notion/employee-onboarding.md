# Free Notion Employee Onboarding Template 2026

This comprehensive employee onboarding template is designed specifically for HR managers who want to streamline their new hire process and ensure consistent, thorough onboarding experiences. The template provides a complete system for managing everything from pre-boarding tasks to 90-day check-ins.

## Who This Template Is For

- HR managers and coordinators
- People operations teams
- Small to medium business owners
- Team leads responsible for onboarding
- Organizations looking to standardize their onboarding process

## Complete Template Structure

### 1. Onboarding Dashboard
**Master Control Center**
- Overview of all active onboarding cases
- Progress tracking with visual indicators
- Quick stats on completion rates
- Upcoming deadlines and overdue tasks
- Filter options by department, start date, and status

### 2. Employee Database
**New Hire Information Hub**
- **Employee Name**: Full name of new hire
- **Position**: Job title and role
- **Department**: Assigned department/team
- **Start Date**: Official first day
- **Manager**: Direct supervisor information
- **Status**: Pre-boarding, Active, Completed
- **Progress**: Percentage completion tracker
- **Contact Info**: Phone, email, emergency contacts
- **Notes**: Additional remarks and observations

### 3. Onboarding Checklist Template
**Comprehensive Task Management**
- **Pre-boarding Tasks** (Week before start)
  - Send welcome email and materials
  - Prepare workspace and equipment
  - Create system accounts
  - Schedule first-day meetings
- **Day 1 Tasks**
  - Office tour and introductions
  - IT setup and account access
  - HR paperwork completion
  - Safety training
- **Week 1 Tasks**
  - Department orientation
  - Role-specific training
  - Buddy system assignment
  - Initial goal setting
- **30-Day Milestones**
  - Performance check-in
  - Feedback collection
  - Training assessment
- **90-Day Review**
  - Comprehensive evaluation
  - Goal reassessment
  - Long-term planning

### 4. Document Library
**Centralized Resource Center**
- **Employee Handbook**: Company policies and procedures
- **Forms Database**: All required paperwork and forms
- **Training Materials**: Role-specific learning resources
- **Templates**: Email templates, presentation materials
- **Compliance Documents**: Legal and regulatory requirements
- **Quick Reference Guides**: System access, contacts, FAQs

### 5. Calendar & Scheduling
**Timeline Management System**
- **Onboarding Calendar**: Visual timeline view
- **Meeting Scheduler**: Automated booking system
- **Deadline Tracker**: Important dates and milestones
- **Reminder System**: Automated notifications
- **Availability Checker**: Stakeholder calendar integration

### 6. Feedback & Assessment
**Quality Assurance Hub**
- **New Hire Surveys**: Experience feedback forms
- **Manager Evaluations**: Supervisor assessment tools
- **Training Effectiveness**: Learning outcome tracking
- **Process Improvement**: Suggestions and iterations
- **Satisfaction Scores**: Quantitative rating system

## Step-by-Step Usage Instructions

### Step 1: Initial Setup
1. Duplicate the template to your Notion workspace
2. Customize company-specific information in the settings
3. Add your team members and assign permissions
4. Upload your existing onboarding documents to the library
5. Configure notification preferences

### Step 2: New Hire Setup
1. Add new employee to the Employee Database
2. Fill in all required personal and role information
3. Assign the appropriate onboarding checklist template
4. Set automated reminders for key milestones
5. Notify relevant team members and managers

### Step 3: Pre-boarding Process
1. Review and complete all pre-boarding tasks
2. Send welcome package using email templates
3. Schedule Day 1 meetings and orientations
4. Prepare workspace and equipment assignments
5. Update progress tracker as tasks are completed

### Step 4: Active Onboarding
1. Guide new hire through Day 1 checklist
2. Track completion of each onboarding milestone
3. Monitor feedback and address any issues
4. Update progress indicators regularly
5. Schedule and conduct check-in meetings

### Step 5: Review and Optimization
1. Collect feedback from new hire and managers
2. Analyze onboarding effectiveness metrics
3. Update processes based on feedback
4. Archive completed onboarding records
5. Refine templates for future use
