# Complete Recruitment Pipeline Tracking Template for HR Managers

This comprehensive Notion template transforms your recruitment process into an organized, efficient pipeline that helps HR managers track candidates, manage job positions, and streamline hiring decisions from initial application to final onboarding.

## What This Template Does

This recruitment pipeline template provides a complete system for managing your hiring process. It includes candidate tracking, position management, interview scheduling, and reporting dashboards that give you full visibility into your recruitment funnel. Perfect for HR managers handling multiple positions simultaneously and needing to maintain organized records of all candidates and hiring activities.

- **HR Managers** overseeing multiple job openings and candidates
- **Talent Acquisition Specialists** managing recruitment pipelines
- **Small to medium business owners** handling their own hiring
- **HR teams** needing centralized candidate tracking
- **Recruiters** wanting to organize their candidate database

## Template Sections and Fields

### 1. Candidates Database
The core database containing all candidate information and tracking data.

**Fields:**
- **Name** (Title): Candidate's full name
- **Position Applied** (Relation): Links to Jobs database
- **Email** (Email): Contact email address
- **Phone** (Phone): Contact number
- **Resume** (Files): Uploaded resume/CV documents
- **Cover Letter** (Files): Cover letter documents
- **Portfolio/LinkedIn** (URL): Professional profiles and work samples
- **Current Stage** (Select): Application Received, Phone Screen, First Interview, Second Interview, Final Interview, Reference Check, Offer Extended, Offer Accepted, Rejected, Withdrawn
- **Source** (Select): Job Board, LinkedIn, Referral, Company Website, Recruiter, Other
- **Application Date** (Date): When application was received
- **Last Activity** (Date): Most recent interaction or update
- **Interviewer** (Person): Assigned interviewer or HR contact
- **Rating** (Select): 1-5 star rating system
- **Notes** (Rich Text): Interview notes and observations
- **Salary Expectation** (Number): Candidate's salary requirements
- **Start Date** (Date): Preferred/actual start date
- **Status** (Formula): Auto-calculates based on stage and dates

### 2. Job Positions Database
Tracks all open positions and their requirements.

**Fields:**
- **Job Title** (Title): Position name
- **Department** (Select): Engineering, Marketing, Sales, HR, Finance, Operations, Other
- **Employment Type** (Select): Full-time, Part-time, Contract, Intern
- **Location** (Select): Remote, Hybrid, On-site
- **Salary Range** (Rich Text): Compensation details
- **Job Description** (Rich Text): Full position description
- **Requirements** (Rich Text): Required skills and qualifications
- **Hiring Manager** (Person): Responsible hiring manager
- **HR Contact** (Person): Primary HR representative
- **Posted Date** (Date): When job was posted
- **Target Close Date** (Date): When position should be filled
- **Status** (Select): Open, On Hold, Filled, Cancelled
- **Priority** (Select): High, Medium, Low
- **Candidates** (Relation): Links to all applicants for this position

### 3. Interview Schedule Database
Manages all interview appointments and logistics.

**Fields:**
- **Interview Title** (Title): Auto-populated with candidate and position
- **Candidate** (Relation): Links to Candidates database
- **Position** (Relation): Links to Jobs database
- **Interview Type** (Select): Phone Screen, Video Call, In-Person, Panel Interview, Technical Interview
- **Date & Time** (Date): Interview schedule
- **Interviewer(s)** (Person): Who's conducting the interview
- **Location/Link** (Rich Text): Meeting room or video call details
- **Duration** (Select): 30 min, 45 min, 1 hour, 1.5 hours, 2 hours
- **Status** (Select): Scheduled, Completed, Cancelled, Rescheduled
- **Preparation Notes** (Rich Text): Questions and preparation items
- **Interview Notes** (Rich Text): Post-interview observations
- **Outcome** (Select): Move Forward, Reject, Needs Discussion

### 4. Recruitment Analytics Dashboard
Visual overview of recruitment metrics and pipeline health.

**Views:**
- **Pipeline Overview**: Kanban board showing candidates by stage
- **This Week's Interviews**: Calendar view of upcoming interviews
- **Position Status**: Gallery view of all open positions
- **Candidate Ratings**: Table sorted by rating and stage
- **Recent Applications**: Timeline of newest candidates

**Key Metrics:**
- Total active candidates
- Candidates by stage
- Average time per stage
- Interview completion rate
- Offer acceptance rate

### 5. Templates and Resources Section
Ready-to-use templates and reference materials.

**Includes:**
- Interview question templates by role type
- Email templates for candidate communication
- Rejection letter templates
- Offer letter template
- Reference check form template
- Onboarding checklist template

## Step-by-Step Instructions

### Getting Started
1. **Duplicate the template** using the link below
2. **Customize job positions** by adding your current open roles
3. **Set up your team** by adding HR managers and interviewers as users
4. **Configure email templates** with your company branding

### Adding New Candidates
1. **Create new candidate record** in the Candidates database
2. **Fill in basic information** (name, email, phone, application date)
3. **Upload resume and documents** to the files fields
4. **Link to applied position** using the relation field
5. **Set initial stage** to "Application Received"
6. **Add source** of application for tracking

### Managing the Pipeline
1. **Review new applications** daily in the Pipeline Overview
2. **Move candidates through stages** by updating the Current Stage field
3. **Schedule interviews** by creating records in Interview Schedule
4. **Update candidate ratings** after each interaction
5. **Add detailed notes** throughout the process

### Interview Management
1. **Create interview record** linked to candidate and position
2. **Set date, time, and interviewer** details
3. **Add preparation notes** and question topics
4. **Send calendar invites** using the provided information
5. **Update with interview notes** and outcomes post-interview

### Reporting and Analytics
1. **Check Pipeline Overview** weekly for bottlenecks
2. **Review metrics** in the Analytics Dashboard
3. **Track time-to-hire** using date fields and formulas
4. **Monitor interviewer workload** through assignment tracking
5. **Generate reports** using filtered views and exports

### Closing Positions
1. **Update successful candidate** stage to "Offer Accepted"
2. **Mark position status** as "Filled"
3. **Update remaining candidates** to appropriate rejection stages
4. **Send closure communications** using email templates
5. **Archive or delete** outdated records as needed

You can modify the "Current Stage" select field in the Candidates database to match your specific hiring workflow. Simply edit the select options to add, remove, or rename stages. The kanban view will automatically update to reflect your custom stages.

### Can multiple HR team members collaborate on this template?
Yes, this template is designed for team collaboration. You can share the workspace with multiple HR managers and assign specific candidates or positions to different team members. The Person fields allow you to track who's responsible for each candidate and interview.
