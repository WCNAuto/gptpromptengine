## Template Sections and Fields

### 1. Deals Database
**Purpose**: Central hub for tracking all sales opportunities

**Fields**:
- **Deal Name** (Title): Descriptive name for the opportunity
- **Company** (Relation): Links to Companies database
- **Contact** (Relation): Primary contact from Contacts database
- **Stage** (Select): Current pipeline stage (Prospecting, Qualifying, Proposal, Negotiation, Closed Won, Closed Lost)
- **Deal Value** (Number): Potential revenue amount
- **Probability** (Formula): Auto-calculated based on stage (Prospecting: 10%, Qualifying: 25%, Proposal: 50%, Negotiation: 75%)
- **Weighted Value** (Formula): Deal Value × Probability
- **Expected Close Date** (Date): Target closing date
- **Created Date** (Created time): When deal was added
- **Last Activity** (Last edited): Most recent update
- **Source** (Select): How the lead was generated (Website, Referral, Cold Outreach, Trade Show, etc.)
- **Next Action** (Text): What needs to happen next
- **Notes** (Rich text): Detailed notes and conversation history
- **Assigned To** (Person): Sales rep responsible for the deal

### 2. Companies Database
**Purpose**: Store detailed information about prospect organizations

**Fields**:
- **Company Name** (Title): Organization name
- **Industry** (Select): Business sector
- **Company Size** (Select): Employee count range
- **Website** (URL): Company website
- **Address** (Text): Physical location
- **Annual Revenue** (Number): Company's yearly revenue
- **Pain Points** (Multi-select): Identified challenges
- **Decision Making Process** (Text): How they make purchasing decisions
- **Budget Range** (Text): Estimated budget availability
- **Deals** (Relation): Connected opportunities
- **Contacts** (Relation): People at this company

### 3. Contacts Database
**Purpose**: Manage individual prospects and their details

**Fields**:
- **Full Name** (Title): Contact's name
- **Company** (Relation): Employer organization
- **Job Title** (Text): Role and department
- **Email** (Email): Primary email address
- **Phone** (Phone): Direct phone number
- **LinkedIn** (URL): Professional profile
- **Role in Decision** (Select): Influencer, Decision Maker, Champion, Gatekeeper
- **Communication Preference** (Select): Email, Phone, LinkedIn, In-Person
- **Last Contact** (Date): Most recent interaction
- **Contact History** (Rich text): Interaction timeline
- **Deals** (Relation): Associated opportunities

### 4. Activities Database
**Purpose**: Log all sales activities and touchpoints

**Fields**:
- **Activity** (Title): Brief description of activity
- **Type** (Select): Call, Email, Meeting, Demo, Proposal, Follow-up
- **Deal** (Relation): Associated opportunity
- **Contact** (Relation): Person involved
- **Date** (Date): When activity occurred
- **Duration** (Number): Time spent in minutes
- **Outcome** (Select): Positive, Neutral, Negative, No Response
- **Next Steps** (Text): Follow-up actions needed
- **Notes** (Rich text): Detailed activity summary

### 5. Sales Dashboard
**Purpose**: Visual overview of pipeline performance

**Components**:
- **Pipeline Overview**: Kanban view of deals by stage
- **Revenue Forecast**: Sum of weighted values by month
- **Deal Velocity**: Average time in each stage
- **Win Rate**: Percentage of deals closed won
- **Activity Summary**: Recent sales activities
- **Top Opportunities**: Highest value deals in progress
- **Upcoming Tasks**: Deals requiring immediate attention

## Frequently Asked Questions

### How do I customize the sales stages for my specific sales process?
Go to any deal record, click on the Stage field, then "Edit property" to modify the select options. You can add, remove, or rename stages to match your sales methodology. Don't forget to update the probability formula if you change the stages significantly.

### Can I track team performance if multiple people use this template?
Yes! The "Assigned To" field in the Deals database allows you to assign deals to different team members. You can create filtered views on the dashboard to show each person's pipeline, and use Notion's built-in analytics to compare performance across team members.

### How do I set up automated reminders for follow-ups?
While this template doesn't include automated notifications, you can set up manual reminders by using the "Next Action" field and creating a filtered view that shows deals requiring follow-up. For automated reminders, consider integrating with tools like Zapier or using Notion's API to connect with your calendar application.
