## FAQ

### Can this template handle multiple sales territories?
Yes! The template includes territory management features where you can assign team members to specific geographic regions or industry verticals. Each territory can have separate quotas, and you can filter all views by territory to see performance by region.

### How does the template calculate sales forecasts?
The forecasting system multiplies deal value by probability percentage for each deal in your pipeline, then aggregates by expected close date. You can view forecasts by month, quarter, or custom date ranges. The system also factors in historical conversion rates to provide more accurate predictions.
