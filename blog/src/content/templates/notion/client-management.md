# Free Client Management Agency Template for Marketing Managers

This comprehensive Notion template is designed specifically for marketing managers and agency professionals who need to streamline their client management processes. Whether you're running a small marketing agency or managing multiple client accounts within a larger organization, this template provides everything you need to keep your clients happy, projects on track, and campaigns performing at their best.

Perfect for marketing managers, agency owners, account managers, and marketing teams who want to centralize client information, track project progress, manage campaigns, and maintain clear communication with clients and team members.

## Template Structure and Sections

### 1. Client Database
**Purpose**: Central hub for all client information and relationship management

**Fields**:
- **Client Name** (Title): Primary client company name
- **Status** (Select): Active, Onboarding, Paused, Churned
- **Industry** (Select): Technology, Healthcare, Finance, E-commerce, etc.
- **Contract Value** (Number): Total contract value in currency
- **Start Date** (Date): When the client relationship began
- **Contract End** (Date): Contract expiration date
- **Primary Contact** (Person): Main point of contact at client company
- **Account Manager** (Person): Internal team member responsible
- **Contact Info** (Rich Text): Phone, email, and other contact details
- **Notes** (Rich Text): Important client information and preferences
- **Related Projects** (Relation): Links to active and completed projects
- **Related Campaigns** (Relation): Links to marketing campaigns

### 2. Projects Database
**Purpose**: Track all client projects from initiation to completion

**Fields**:
- **Project Name** (Title): Descriptive project title
- **Client** (Relation): Links to client database
- **Status** (Select): Planning, In Progress, Review, Completed, On Hold
- **Priority** (Select): High, Medium, Low
- **Project Type** (Select): Website, Branding, Campaign, Strategy, Content
- **Start Date** (Date): Project kickoff date
- **Due Date** (Date): Project deadline
- **Budget** (Number): Allocated project budget
- **Progress** (Number): Percentage completion (0-100%)
- **Assigned Team** (Multi-person): Team members working on project
- **Description** (Rich Text): Detailed project scope and objectives
- **Deliverables** (Rich Text): List of expected outputs
- **Files & Assets** (Files): Project-related documents and media

### 3. Campaigns Database
**Purpose**: Monitor marketing campaign performance and metrics

**Fields**:
- **Campaign Name** (Title): Campaign title or identifier
- **Client** (Relation): Associated client
- **Campaign Type** (Select): Social Media, PPC, Email, Content, SEO
- **Status** (Select): Planning, Active, Paused, Completed
- **Start Date** (Date): Campaign launch date
- **End Date** (Date): Campaign conclusion date
- **Budget** (Number): Total campaign budget
- **Spent** (Number): Amount spent to date
- **Platform** (Multi-select): Facebook, Google, LinkedIn, Instagram, etc.
- **Target Audience** (Rich Text): Demographic and psychographic details
- **Objectives** (Rich Text): Campaign goals and KPIs
- **Performance Notes** (Rich Text): Results and optimization notes
- **ROI** (Formula): Return on investment calculation

### 4. Tasks Database
**Purpose**: Manage day-to-day tasks and action items across all clients

**Fields**:
- **Task Name** (Title): Clear, actionable task description
- **Status** (Select): Not Started, In Progress, Waiting, Completed
- **Priority** (Select): Urgent, High, Medium, Low
- **Assigned To** (Person): Team member responsible
- **Client** (Relation): Related client (if applicable)
- **Project** (Relation): Related project (if applicable)
- **Due Date** (Date): Task deadline
- **Estimated Hours** (Number): Time estimate for completion
- **Actual Hours** (Number): Time actually spent
- **Category** (Select): Meeting, Content, Design, Strategy, Admin
- **Description** (Rich Text): Task details and requirements
- **Comments** (Rich Text): Progress updates and notes

### 5. Meetings Database
**Purpose**: Schedule and track client meetings and internal discussions

**Fields**:
- **Meeting Title** (Title): Meeting subject or agenda topic
- **Date & Time** (Date): Meeting schedule
- **Client** (Relation): Associated client
- **Meeting Type** (Select): Kickoff, Status Update, Strategy, Review
- **Attendees** (Multi-person): Internal and external participants
- **Location/Link** (URL): Physical location or video call link
- **Agenda** (Rich Text): Meeting topics and objectives
- **Notes** (Rich Text): Meeting minutes and action items
- **Follow-up Required** (Checkbox): Indicates if follow-up is needed
- **Recording** (Files): Meeting recording or presentation files

### 6. Team Members Database
**Purpose**: Manage team capacity, skills, and workload

**Fields**:
- **Name** (Title): Team member full name
- **Role** (Select): Account Manager, Designer, Developer, Strategist, etc.
- **Email** (Email): Contact information
- **Skills** (Multi-select): Areas of expertise
- **Current Workload** (Select): Light, Moderate, Heavy, Overloaded
- **Hourly Rate** (Number): Billing rate for projects
- **Active Projects** (Relation): Currently assigned projects
- **Availability** (Rich Text): Schedule and time-off information

## Frequently Asked Questions

### Q: Can I customize the select options and fields to match my agency's specific services?
**A:** Absolutely! This template is designed to be fully customizable. You can modify select options (like project types, industries, or campaign types), add new fields, or remove ones you don't need. Simply go to any database, click the "..." menu, and select "Properties" to make changes. You can also add new views and filters to match your workflow.

### Q: How do I track profitability and billing for each client and project?
**A:** The template includes budget fields for both clients and projects, plus time tracking capabilities in the Tasks database. You can calculate profitability by comparing the project budget against the actual hours spent (multiplied by team member hourly rates). For more advanced financial tracking, consider creating additional formula fields or connecting with your existing accounting software.

### Q: Can multiple team members collaborate on this template simultaneously?
**A:** Yes! Notion allows real-time collaboration, so your entire team can use this template simultaneously. Each team member can have their own filtered views, be assigned to specific tasks and projects, and update progress in real-time. Make sure to share the template with appropriate permissions for your team members based on their roles and responsibilities.
