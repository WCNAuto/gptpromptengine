# Free Notion HR Manager Template 2026

This comprehensive HR management template is designed specifically for HR managers, HR business partners, and people operations professionals who want to streamline their human resources processes in one centralized Notion workspace. Whether you're managing a team of 10 or 1000+ employees, this template provides everything you need to track employees, manage recruitment, conduct performance reviews, and maintain HR compliance.

Perfect for HR managers at startups, scale-ups, and established companies looking to digitize their HR operations without expensive HRIS software.

## Template Sections & Fields

### 📊 HR Dashboard
- **Key Metrics Overview**: Employee count, open positions, pending reviews, onboarding progress
- **Quick Stats Cards**: Headcount by department, diversity metrics, retention rates
- **Recent Activity Feed**: Latest hires, departures, and important HR updates
- **Action Items**: Overdue tasks, upcoming deadlines, and priority items

### 👥 Employee Database
- **Personal Information**: Full name, employee ID, email, phone, emergency contact
- **Employment Details**: Start date, position, department, manager, employment type
- **Compensation**: Salary, bonus structure, equity, benefits enrollment
- **Status Tracking**: Active, on leave, terminated, probation
- **Documents**: Contract links, ID copies, certifications
- **Performance History**: Review scores, promotion dates, disciplinary actions

### 🎯 Recruitment Pipeline
- **Job Postings**: Position title, department, job description, requirements, salary range
- **Candidate Tracking**: Name, contact info, source, application date, current stage
- **Interview Process**: Scheduled interviews, interviewer assignments, feedback forms
- **Pipeline Stages**: Applied, screening, interview, offer, hired, rejected
- **Hiring Metrics**: Time to hire, cost per hire, source effectiveness

### 📋 Performance Management
- **Review Cycles**: Annual, quarterly, mid-year review scheduling
- **Employee Goals**: SMART goals, progress tracking, completion status
- **360 Reviews**: Peer feedback, manager assessments, self-evaluations
- **Performance Ratings**: Overall score, competency ratings, improvement areas
- **Development Plans**: Skill gaps, training recommendations, career progression

### 🚀 Onboarding Workflow
- **Pre-boarding Checklist**: Contract signing, background checks, equipment prep
- **First Day Tasks**: Office tour, system access, introductions, handbook review
- **30-60-90 Day Plans**: Role-specific training, goal setting, check-in meetings
- **Buddy System**: Mentor assignments, meeting schedules, feedback collection
- **Progress Tracking**: Completion status, feedback scores, manager notes

### 📚 Training & Development
- **Training Programs**: Course catalog, mandatory training, skill development
- **Learning Paths**: Role-based curricula, certification requirements
- **Training Records**: Completion dates, scores, certificates earned
- **Budget Tracking**: Training expenses, budget allocation, ROI metrics
- **External Training**: Conference attendance, course approvals, reimbursements

### 🏖️ Leave Management
- **Leave Requests**: Employee name, leave type, dates, approval status
- **Leave Balances**: Vacation, sick, personal, parental leave accruals
- **Holiday Calendar**: Company holidays, office closures, floating holidays
- **Coverage Plans**: Temporary assignments, handover documentation
- **Compliance Tracking**: FMLA, state leave laws, documentation requirements

### 📊 HR Analytics
- **Turnover Analysis**: Departure reasons, department trends, exit interview data
- **Diversity Metrics**: Demographics breakdown, pay equity analysis, representation goals
- **Engagement Scores**: Survey results, pulse check data, action plans
- **Cost Analysis**: Hiring costs, training expenses, benefits utilization
- **Productivity Metrics**: Performance trends, goal achievement rates

## Frequently Asked Questions

**Q: Can this template handle a large number of employees?**
A: Yes, this template is designed to scale with your organization. Notion databases can handle thousands of records, and the template includes filtering and sorting options to manage large datasets efficiently. For organizations with 500+ employees, consider breaking down the employee database by department or region for optimal performance.

**Q: Is this template GDPR and privacy compliant?**
A: The template structure supports privacy compliance by allowing you to control access permissions and data visibility. However, you'll need to configure Notion's sharing settings appropriately, implement data retention policies, and ensure you have proper consent for storing employee data. Always consult with your legal team for specific compliance requirements.

**Q: Can I integrate this template with other HR tools and systems?**
A: While this template works as a standalone system, you can enhance it by integrating with other tools through Notion's API or third-party automation platforms like Zapier. Common integrations include connecting with your ATS for candidate data, payroll systems for compensation updates, and survey tools for engagement metrics.
